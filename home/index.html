Bold flavours, authentic ingredients, made with meraki

Meraki Souq was born from a deep love of Lebanese culture and food — rooted in the recipes of Sittie Maloof and the warmth of family tradition.

The name says it all: Meraki means to do something with love, passion, and creativity. Souq is Arabic for marketplace. Every dish we prepare carries the flavors of Sittie Maloof's wonderful Lebanese kitchen — generous, fresh, and made with care.

Got Questions

Frequently asked questions

If you have further questions, don't hesitate to call us at (706) 851-0280 or reach out by email.

Do you accept walk-ins?
+
Yes! We welcome walk-ins during all our open hours. No reservation needed — just come hungry.
What are your opening hours?
+
We're open Monday & Sunday 11am–2pm, and Tuesday through Saturday 11am–5pm. Hours may vary — check Google or call us to confirm.
Do you offer takeout or delivery?
+
Yes! You can order online through our Square site for carry-out. We also offer delivery through select platforms — check our order page for current options.
Is the food made fresh daily?
+
Absolutely. Every dish — from our hummus to our tabbouleh to our pita — is made fresh the day it's served. We never reheat yesterday's food.
Do you have vegetarian options?
+
Yes, many of our dishes are naturally vegetarian — hummus, tabbouleh, labne, fattoush, pita, and much of our grocery selection. Just ask us and we'll guide you.
Do you carry specialty grocery items?
+
Yes! Meraki Souq carries specialty Middle Eastern grocery items, spices, pantry goods, and imported products alongside our fresh food.
Where are you located?
+
We're at 303 Main St., Ducktown, TN 37326 — come find us in the heart of Ducktown!
